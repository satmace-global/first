Material Journey :

The material journey in recycling is the process by which recyclable materials are collected, sorted, and processed into new products. The journey begins when you put your recyclables in the bin. From there, they are collected by a waste hauler and taken to a Material Recovery Facility (MRF). At the MRF, the recyclables are sorted by type and baled. The bales are then sold to end-users, who use them to make new products.
